Q1) Do I need to pay to apply to a job or get an interview call? / Recruiters are asking me to pay to schedule interview for job?

No. You can apply to jobs for FREE on Vakarien.
Note that genuine recruiters do not ask for money to schedule interviews or offer a job. If you are receiving such calls or emails, beware as this might be a job scam.
We regret that sometimes, despite our best efforts, false job postings are listed online, and non-existing job offers are sent via email to illegally collect personal information and/or money from unsuspecting job seekers. Please note that Vakarien.com does not approve of, or represent any employer or recruiter sending such fraudulent communication, which in fact are a violation of the Terms and Conditions. In such cases, we remain vigilant every day - When we receive complaints from Jobseekers about fraudulent or suspicious emails, we promptly notify the concerned employer client and, if necessary, block them from using our services.


Q2) I do not want my current employer to have access to my profile. How can I block a recruiter?

You have an option to select companies to whom you do not wish to reveal your resume. Once you have logged in to your account, click on ‘My Profile’ tab visible at the top right corner of the page.
On mobile, you can find this in the side menu bar. Click in the above given section. Here, you can search and select the company that you want to block. It may be your current employer or any other company. If you cannot find the company name in the list, search for variants.
For example, ABC Private Limited & ABC Pvt. Ltd are variants of the same company name. Recruiters from the companies selected will not be able to view your profile. We will try our best to conceal you profile from these companies but we cannot guarantee this.


Q3) How can I delete my Vakarien account?

Note that once you delete your Vakarien account, you cannot get your Vakarien profile back. For security reasons, you also may not be able to create a new account with the email used by you earlier.
Job Search is not a one-time activity. Your Vakarien profile might be useful when you search for a new job in the future, hence we don’t recommend users to delete their profiles.

  1. If you wish to delete your account because you already got a job, you can deactivate your account instead. Deactivating it would put your account in sleep mode with no notifications until the next time you login on Vakarien.
  2. If you want to delete your account because you are not getting relevant job recommendations, try creating a Custom Job Alert. Read more about custom job alert here
  3. If you want to delete your account because you want to stop emails from Vakarien, try changing your communication settings to stop all emails and mobile notifications. You can still search and apply to jobs but you not receive any emails from Vakarien. Learn how to stop all emails from Vakarien here.


Q4) How can I deactivate my Vakarien account?

In case you are not looking for a job right now, you can deactivate your Vakarien account. This will stop all emails from Vakarien. We will reactivate your account only when you login again on Vakarien.com.
To deactivate your Vakarien account, go to ‘Communication and Privacy’ in the ‘Settings’ page. Here, select ‘Not looking for job right now’ and select ‘Deactivate till next login”.


Q5) How can I apply for a job on Vakarien?

You can search for jobs that have been posted by companies and recruiters on Vakarien based on key skills, job title/designation or company names. You can also specify location, minimum work experience and minimum salary expected, before searching. If you get hundreds of jobs, you can narrow down your search by using filters.
If you do not want to apply to a job right away, you can save the job and apply later. If we show, “No Results Found” for your search result, we recommend you to broaden the search by reducing your filters and criteria while also checking for spelling mistakes in your query.
Applying for jobs on Vakarien is simple. To apply for jobs

  1. Register on Vakarien to create a Vakarien profile.
  2. Fill details in your Vakarien profile. These details will be shown to the recruiter when you apply for a job. Based on these details, the recruiter may shortlist you for an interview.
  3. Search for jobs. Click on a job posting to which you would like to apply.
  4. Click Apply button to apply to the job with your Vakarien profile.
  5. If the recruiter needs some extra information before accepting your application, a questionnaire will be displayed. Fill this questionnaire for your application to be successful.
  6. If you see Apply on Company Website button, you cannot apply for this job with your Vakarien profile. You will be taken to the company’s career page where you may be asked to fill out an application online


Q6) How do I search for jobs on Vakarien?

You can search for jobs that have been posted by companies and recruiters on Vakarien based on key skills, job title/designation or company names. You can also specify location, minimum work experience and minimum salary expected, before searching. If you get hundreds of jobs, you can narrow down your search by using filters. If you do not want to apply to a job right away, you can save the job and apply later. If we show, “No Results Found” for your search result, we recommend you to broaden the search by reducing your filters and criteria while also checking for spelling mistakes in your query.

  1. Go to Vakarien.com and click on the search bar at the top.
  2. In the search bar, type a keyskill, job designation or company name. You can also use Boolean search to refine your search query.
  3. You can type multiple terms in the search bar. An example would be search as “java, javascript, software engineer” which combines both keyskills such as ‘java’ and ‘javascript’ with the designation ‘software engineer’. This would look for all three terms to display job listings.
  4. Type the location where you would like to get a job in the location field. Select the work experience in years for which you want to see jobs in the work experience field. Select the minimum salary for which you would like to see jobs.
  5. Click Search.


Q7) How do I know if a call or email from Vakarien is fake or genuine?

Please note that Vakarien.com doesn’t ask you to pay for applying to jobs or for getting an interview. We are neither a recruitment firm nor a labor consultant company. We are a platform that connects job seekers and employers. Searching for jobs and applying for jobs on Vakarien.com is free for all job seekers.
To identify genuine emails from Vakarien.com, pay attention to the ‘From’ email address. Vakarien.com email addresses always end in a @vakarien.com domain name. For example, resdex@vakarien.com, vakarienalerts@vakarien.com, info@vakarien.com are Vakarien email addresses.
If the ‘From’ email address looks like vakarienfastforward@jobs4u.com, vakarien@onevakarien.com, recruitmentvakarien@gmail.com, etc., then the email is not sent by Vakarien.com.
